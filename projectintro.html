URBAN MANAGEMENT IN CHANDIGARH

Course Project:SMART CITY DESIGN...

Overview

Our project aims to design a sustainable Smart City plan for Chandigarh, integrating urban and peripheral areas. The goal is to create a technologically advanced, E-governance system, disaster management,Pure Water Distribution,Health Monitoring and Emergency,Green Waste Management,Smart Tourist Navigation and addresses urban management challenges through innovative solutions while preserving Chandigarh's cultural and environmental heritage.

Key Features:

  • Leverage advanced algorithms for efficient urban planning and resource management in Chandigarh.
  • Use data-driven insights to optimize traffic flow, utilities, and public services.
  • Integrate modern technologies to enhance sustainability and foster active citizen engagement in the city's development.

By combining efficient algorithms and data structures, we aim to develop solutions that are innovative and impactful.

Problem Space and How It Was Arrived At ??..

The problem space for urban management in Chandigarh revolves around addressing challenges from rapid urbanization, environmental concerns, and strained infrastructure. As India's first planned city, its robust design now struggles with traffic congestion, inadequate public transport, and rising pollution levels. Environmental issues like deforestation, poor waste management, and aging infrastructure highlight the need for sustainable solutions. Affordable housing shortages lead to unplanned settlements, while governance challenges and limited public participation slow project implementation. Despite smart city aspirations, slow adoption of digital tools and data-driven approaches hinders progress. These issues, identified through studies, consultations, and feedback, emphasize the need for a sustainable, citizen-centric urban management strategy.
