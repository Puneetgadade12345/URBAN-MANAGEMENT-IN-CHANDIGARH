Course Introduction

This course centers on the design and analysis of algorithms, emphasizing their real-world applications. The primary objective is to equip students with the skills to design algorithms tailored to specific tasks, implement them effectively using appropriate data structures, and optimize their efficiency. By bridging theoretical foundations with practical applications, this course provides essential tools for solving a wide range of computational problems.

Business Cases Identified by Each Team Member

Team Member 1: Traffic Management

Developing algorithms to optimize traffic flow in Chandigarh by leveraging real-time data from sensors and cameras. The goal is to reduce congestion and minimize travel times through dynamic signal adjustments and route recommendations.

Team Member 2: Waste Management

Implementing smart bin systems that use IoT-enabled devices to monitor waste levels. The algorithm will prioritize waste collection routes to ensure timely disposal and improve sanitation efficiency in urban areas.

Team Member 3: Energy Optimization

Designing algorithms to analyze energy consumption patterns in Chandigarh's urban areas. The focus is on proposing efficient energy usage strategies and integrating renewable energy sources for sustainable city management.

Team Member 4: Public Safety

Creating a real-time monitoring and alert system using algorithms that process surveillance data. The system aims to detect anomalies, identify potential threats, and enhance public safety measures in Chandigarh.
