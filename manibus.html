health Monitoring

SDG 3: SDG 3 is Implementing systems to track and improve individual and public health metrics, ensuring timely healthcare interventions..

Health monitoring systems play a critical role in achieving SDG 3, which aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. By leveraging technology for real-time health tracking, early diagnosis, and preventive care, these systems can: Reduce maternal and child mortality. Combat epidemics like COVID-19, malaria, and tuberculosis. Promote mental health and well-being.
